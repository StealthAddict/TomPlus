ABOUT ME

  • In The Beginning

    February 2013

    At the young age of 9, Tom creates his first YouTube channel, Channelnutpig.

  • Dawn of the Twitch Streamer

    September 2018

    Tom begins his Twitch career by regularly streaming games like PUBG, Fortnite, and Minecraft Hypixel.

  • Taking The First Steps

    Early 2019

    With aide of his energetic personality, Tom becomes an affiliate with Twitch. Soon after, he begins to gain traction on his Tommyinnit YouTube channel as he posts Hypixel Skyblock videos.

  • Joining the Community

    November 2019

    Tom joins EarthSMP, a Minecraft multiplayer server full of popular Minecraft streamers and YouTubers. Here he is quick to make friends and "enemies" on his journey to become the (not very) profitable nation of Business Bay.

  • Breaking the Sound Barrier

    July 2020

    The next few months are some of the biggest for Tom. Starting on July, he joins the DreamSMP, a Minecraft multiplayer server populated by friends of the content creator Dream. With the aid of his partner in crime, WilburSoot, the DreamSMP skyrockets in fame for its Hamilton inspired storyline. This is Tom's rise in popularity gaining him over 4 million subscribers in the span of just 4 months.

  • Reaching the Top...

    January 2021

    Streaming the finale of a storyline for the DreamSMP, Tom reaches peak viewership of over 650,000 viewers on Twitch, making the stream the third all time high of concurrent Twitch viewers on a stream.

  • ...And Going Beyond

    July 2021

    Just a year after his initial gain of a million subscribers on YouTube, Tom surpasses the 10 million mark. His content has expanded from Minecraft videos and streams to also including vlogs of the adventures he has with friends in the UK.

  • In The Moment

    Today

    Today Tom has amassed over 11 million subscribers on his main YouTube channel and 7 million followers on Twitch. His bright and energetic personality has made him a favorite in his communities, and he continues to take joy in content creation.
